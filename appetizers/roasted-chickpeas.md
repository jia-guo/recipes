# Roasted Chickpeas

## Ingredients

+ 1 can chickpeas
+ 1 tbsp olive oil
+ salt, pepper
+ Preheated oven at 350

## Instructions

1. Drain and wash the chickpeas.
2. Pat down to dry with a paper towel.
3. Mix with olive oil, salt, and pepper in a baking sheet.
4. Place in oven for 20 minutes or desired crispiness.
